Why this Project?

We decided to do this project because, although there are numerous websites that offer restaurant recommendations today, none of them include information about local parking, the number of tables that have been reserved for that evening, or a random selection. These factors were our main considerations while developing the system because, in our opinion, they are crucial when selecting a restaurant.

What was done?

Our website concentrates on two key features for choosing restaurants:
1. Random selection - The user is given three questions, and the system will choose the best restaurant for them based on their responses.
2. Standard selection - The user enters the name of the city and is then presented with a list of restaurants. They are able to pick one and read more about it.
