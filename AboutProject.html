Why this Project

We choose to do this project because in today's market there are many websites that recommend restaurants, but they do not have the options to see if there are nearby parking spaces in the area, the number of reserved tables for that evening and the random selection. In our opinion, these are important things for choosing a restaurant, so we focused on them when we created the system.

What was done?

In our website focused on 2 main functions for restaurant selection: 1.Random selection - The user gets 3 questions and according the answers the system will automatically find the best restaurant for him. 2.Regular selection - The user provides name of city and after that he will get a list of restaurants. He can choose one of them and get information about it.
